## Overview

This video is a book announcement for the "Certified Kubernetes Administrator Companion Guide" by Piyush Sachdeva. It covers:
- Why the book was written (CKA curriculum changes in Feb 2025)
- The research process (30+ interviews with recent exam takers)
- Book structure: 23 chapters in 6 parts covering troubleshooting, workloads, cluster management, logging, alerting, and maintenance
- Appendix with real exam-style sample questions
- Practice tasks after each chapter

### Key Points
- CKA exam is performance-based, 15-20 tasks in 2 hours
- New topics added to curriculum: HPA, VPA, StorageClass, Gateway API, CRD, Operators, Kustomize, Helm
- Topics removed: etcd backup and restore
- Troubleshooting now accounts for 30% of the exam
- Book validated through 30+ interviews with recent exam takers post-Feb 2025 changes
- Available on Amazon (Kindle and paperback)
